Introduction

We were incorporated in the year 2016 by the name of M/s Cool Channel primarily serving our Clients in the field of HVAC. As we progressed gaining trust and loyalty, our Clients granted us opportunities to expand into the broad-spectrum MEP field of works. To cater this need, we started MEP Star.

MEP Star is an Electro-Mechanical service and solutions provider. We specialize in HVAC, PHE, Fire Fighting, Electrical, ELV, MEP Service, Maintenance, AMC’s, Testing, Balancing & Commissioning of all MEP services.

“MEP Star is a one stop shop for all your MEP needs”

We undertake MEP sub-contracts and have a talented young team of Engineers, Supervisors and Technicians for all MEP trades. Besides direct orders from Developers, we also provide technical and manpower services to large MEP contractors such as M/s Sterling & Wilson Pvt. Ltd and M/s Shinryo Suvidha Engineers India Pvt. Ltd.

Our team is well experienced across projects of Industrial, Commercial, Hospitality, Pharmacy, Hospital, and IT sector.
