Fire Fighting and Prevention Services

Installation, Testing & Commissioning

We undertake the supply, installation, testing and commissioning for all Electrical and ELV systems as detailed under:

  • Equipment: We install all kinds of fire pumps on emergency power, diesel pumps, and jockey pumps.
  • Firefighting Systems: Installation of all fire hydrant piping, hose reel piping, and sprinkler piping including hydrants, breaching inlets, hose reels, sprinkler systems, zone control valves, etc.
  • Piping: Our team of pipe fitters, fabricators, and welders handles all types of piping systems for firefighting such as sprinkler systems, fire hydrant piping, hose reel piping, plant room piping, valve assemblies, and all ancillary works.
  • Fire Alarm Systems: Installation of Fire Alarm systems with full integration into other MEP services.
  • TAB: Testing, Adjusting and Balancing of all firefighting and protection systems are done by us.
