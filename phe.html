PHE (Plumbing & Drainage) Services

Installation, Testing & Commissioning

We undertake the supply, installation, testing and commissioning for Plumbing and Drainage works as detailed under:

  • Equipment: We install all kinds of pumping stations, transfer pumps, booster pumps, sectional tanks, GRP tanks, etc.
  • Water Supply and Drainage Systems: Our experienced team of pipe fitters and plumbers are skilled in UPVC, CPVC, PPR, PEX, Cast Iron, HDPE, and more, supervised by well-trained chargehands and supervisors.
  • CP, Sanitary & Drain Fixtures: Installation of all types of delicate and modern chrome plated fittings and ceramic ware. We also handle installation of water meters, manifolds, floor drains, gullies, channel gratings, cleanouts, etc.
  • Underground Drainage: We carry out all underground drainage works and connections to manholes and septic tanks.
  • Steam Piping & Fuel Piping: We undertake the installation of steam piping and fuel piping works.
  • TAB: Testing, Adjusting and Balancing of all plumbing and drainage systems are done by us.
