Electrical & ELV Services

Installation, Testing & Commissioning

We undertake the supply, installation, testing and commissioning for Electrical and ELV systems as detailed below:

  • Equipment: Installation of electrical and ELV low side equipment such as LV panels, starter panels, DBs, DDC panels, monitor/control modules, fire alarm panels, etc.
  • Electrical Systems: Our experienced electricians and supervisors handle all electrical cabling, cable tray fixing, point wiring, and installation of switch sockets.
  • ELV Systems: Complete installation of ELV systems like BMS, CCTV, Access Control, FA/PA, BGM, and AV systems, including field wiring and full system integration.
  • TAB: Testing, Adjusting and Balancing of all electrical and ELV systems are performed by our skilled team.
