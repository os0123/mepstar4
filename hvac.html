HVAC Services

Installation, Testing & Commissioning

Central Plant

We undertake the supply, installation, testing and commissioning for Low-Side of central HVAC works as detailed under:

  • AC Equipment: We install low side AC equipment like FCU, AHU, Pumps, ventilation fans, etc.
  • Sheet Metal Ducting: Our team includes experienced duct fitters, fabricators and installers, supervised by well-trained chargehands and supervisors.
  • MS Chilled Water Piping: We do MS piping for chilled water and condenser water lines using all types of joints including welded, threaded and grooved (Victaulic) fittings. Plant room works including chiller, cooling tower connections, pump connections and valve stations are also handled.
  • Insulation: We handle all types of duct thermal, acoustic, wall, underdeck, and pipe insulation works.
  • TAB: Testing, Adjusting and Balancing of all Central AC systems are performed by us.
DX AC Units

We also undertake the supply, installation, testing and commissioning for DX system AC works as follows:

  • AC Equipment: We handle VRV / VRF systems, Split units, Package units, etc., along with complete copper piping installation. All associated ducting and insulation works are part of our DX services.
  • TAB: Testing, Adjusting and Balancing of all Central AC systems are done by us.
